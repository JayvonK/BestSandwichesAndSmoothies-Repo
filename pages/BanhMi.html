Whatâ€™s a Banh Mi?

You may be wondering what a banh mi is? Well a banh mi is basically a short baguette with a very nice crisp crust and soft texture, but Vietnamese style. The sandwich is usually split in half and filled with savory ingredients. A banh mi usually consists of a fusion of meats and vegetables from native Vietnamese cuisine. For example cha lua which is Vietnamese sausage, cilantro, cucumber , pickled carrots, pickled daikon, red chilli and mayonnaise.
