A Little Bit About Best Sandwiches

Welcome to Best Sandwiches & Smoothies, where Banh mi is not just a sandwich but a culinary masterpiece that tells the tale of Vietnam's rich flavors. Nestled in the heart of 3330 E Hammer Ln Ste C Stockton, CA 95212, our restaurant is a homage to the bustling street food scene of Hanoi. At Best Sandwiches & Smoothies, we take pride in curating an authentic experience, from the crackling crust of our freshly baked baguettes to the savory dance of traditional Vietnamese ingredients within.
